Filosofer

Seneca: Stoicism (4-65)

Life is very short and anxious for those who forget the past, neglect the present, and fear the future.

Marcus Aurelius: Stoicism (121-180)

If you find something very difficult to achieve yourself, don’t imagine it impossible—for anything possible and proper for another person can be achieved as easily by you.

Friedrich Nietzsche: Existentialism (1844-1900)

Battle not with monsters, lest ye become a monster, and if you gaze into the abyss, the abyss gazes also into you.

Osho: Existentialism (1931-1990)

Whether you are a respected member of the community or just a stray dog, it makes no difference - death comes and makes everybody equal.

Bryan Magee: Historically (1930-2019)

The basic drive behind real philosophy is curiosity about the world, not interest in the writings of philosophers.

Chuck Palahniuk: Absurd-Realism (1962-)

We have no Great War. No Great Depression. Our Great War's a spiritual war. Our Great Depression is our lives. We've all been raised on television to believe that one day we'd all be millionaires, and movie gods, and rock stars. But we won't. And we're slowly learning that fact. And we're very, very pissed off.
